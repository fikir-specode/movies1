Batman and Superman are two of the most iconic superheroes in comic book history, each representing very different ideals and approaches to justice. Superman, with his incredible powers like super strength, flight, and heat vision, symbolizes hope, truth, and the embodiment of the American ideal. He operates openly as a symbol of inspiration for humanity. Batman, on the other hand, is a human without superpowers who relies on his intelligence, detective skills, martial arts, and advanced technology to fight crime.
